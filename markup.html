Take your LooK Watch on every adventure! This standalone smartwatch is equipped with worldwide connectivity, so you can make direct phone calls wherever you go without needing to use your cellphone.

It has Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, heart rate sensors, a built-in SOS button, GPS, maps, accelerometers, compass sensors, gyroscope, an advanced quad-core processor and is water resistant.

Bring the LooK Watch to the gym, the office, or around the world. It is a health and fitness tracker that monitors your heart rate, steps travelled, records fitness activities and jogging routes through its LocationNow app. You also have the ability to set health medication reminders at anytime through the LooK Watch, or customize its sensor settings for your active lifestyle.

Stream music on-the-go or store thousands of songs on this smartwatch. With its 16GB storage memory, you can save photos and download your favourite shows or movies. LooK Watch is compatible with Google Play Store. Feel the freedom of downloading your favourite apps straight from the watch.

Looking for a career with the most innovative IoT company in the world? You’ve come to the right place! Here are five reasons why you should consider to join Laipac:

We are a Solid Team that Grows

The team at Laipac is growing. Our technology has spurred plenty of growth, especially with customized applications from our customers that require long term support. Our team is full of innovative, hardworking members. Whether you’re in sales, software, hardware, apps, Android OS, looking to do some incredible things that will change how the things work, you’re welcome to join our team.

We Are Multicultural – But One Family

Laipac is proud of our multicultural heritage. Differences make us strong. Our multicultural team is filled with talented engineers and employees from around the globe. Having multiple points of view and a strong sense of cultural oneness, It makes us family.

We Are Very Innovative with Great Ideas

Laipac is the Innovator of the Year 2018 winner by the city of Richmond Hill. When you work at Laipac, you work with great ideas. Innovative people with great ideas makes the perfect match with Laipac. We are always open to hear your ideas.

We Love To Make Products That Keep People Safe

Laipac is dedicated to safety – to protection. Our products help to keep people safe, and help to protect valuable assets, giving our customers peace of mind. When you work at Laipac, you can know that your knowledge, hard work, and expertise are working towards something good. Protecting people is what we do.

We Are In The Best City – Toronto, Canada

Multiple journals, magazines, newspapers, and other publications have rated Toronto as the best place to live in the world – and it’s hard for us to disagree. This beautiful city is where Laipac makes our headquarters, and if you’re looking for a wonderful, fun place to live with friendly people and plenty to do, you won’t find a better city than Toronto.

Apply Today

If you think you’re a great fit for a position at Laipac, please contact us today. We’d love to hear from you.
